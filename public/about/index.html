### About I'm a composer and programmer in Kansas City. #### Background I got my first bass guitar in 1996. Then formed several bands, cut some records, and toured the US a few times. After earning a B.M., I recorded music for seven years. I continued writing, and my works were performed and awarded at conferences across the US. I was the only composer at the Society of Composer's 2010 National Conference that had two compositions featured. Eventually I was burnt-out on recording and obsessed with programming. I got my first gig at a .NET shop, then moved on to javascript-heavy applications. * [recording credits](http://jcpst.com/credits) * [applications I use](http://jcpst.com/software) [back to jcpst.com](http://jcpst.com)
